FIG. 9701 Air Relief Valve – Threaded NPT – UL / FM
Air release valves permit trapped air to be released from piping systems without losing water pressure. The small orifice releases air under pressure during normal pipeline operation. The float is connected to the vent via a linkage mechanism which is capable of operating the vent under full pipeline pressure. Rated at 300 psi. UL Listed, FM Approved. Fusion Bonded Epoxy Coated Interior and Exterior to AWWA C550 Standard.
