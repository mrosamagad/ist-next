Fireguard produces various types fire fighting hoses with high quality for most demanding situations. All hoses are manufactured according to BS 6391-1983 TYPE 1 & TYPE 2. The Fireguard Model 107 & 108 synthetic fire hose is widely used by firefighters worldwide. This hose features: tough durable and abrasion resistant, supplied with different couplings. The all synthetic construction makes rot proof and easy to clean
