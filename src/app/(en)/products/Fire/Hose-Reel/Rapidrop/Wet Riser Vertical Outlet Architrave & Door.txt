The DRC004W & DRC005W vertical outlet cabinets are designed to be recessed into the wall for easy access to the wet riser valve. The cabinet has a 38mm surrounding flange and is available in red RAL3002 (DRC004W) or in Stainless Steel (DRC005W) finish.

DRC004W cabinet is painted with Epoxy RAL3002 powder paint with UV protection to prevent colour fading.

The cabinets have a wired Georgian glass door complete with a slam shut lock and stainless steel piano hinge.
