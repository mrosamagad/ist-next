UL Listed Pressure Relief Valve
Limits inlet pressure by relieving excess pressure. Operates over a wide flow range. Pressure setting is adjustable with a single screw. Quick opening with adjustable closing speed. Flanged ends to EN 1092-2 PN-16, ANSI B16.1, Class 125, ASME B16.42, Class 150 RF / FF. Grooved Ends to AWWA C606. UL Listed 1.5” to 8” Sizes.
