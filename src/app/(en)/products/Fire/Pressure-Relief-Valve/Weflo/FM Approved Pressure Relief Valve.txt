FM Approved Pressure Relief Valve
Limits inlet pressure by relieving excess pressure. Operates over a wide flow range. Pressure setting is adjustable with a single screw. Inlet pressure adjustment 65 – 180 psi. Quick opening with adjustable closing speed. Flanged ends to EN 1092-2 PN-16, ANSI B16.1, Class 125, ASME B16.42, Class 150 RF / FF. Grooved Ends to AWWA C606. FM Approved 2”-12” Sizes.
