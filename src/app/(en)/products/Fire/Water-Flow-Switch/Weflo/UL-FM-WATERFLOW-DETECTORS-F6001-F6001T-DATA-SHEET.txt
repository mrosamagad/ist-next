UL / FM / CE Listed / Certified / Approved Waterflow Detectors
The Weflo UL / FM / CE Listed / Certified / Approved Waterflow Detectors are a Vane Type design used in fire protection piping systems.  The Figure 6001 is designed for both indoor and outdoor use, with a temperature range of 0° C to 60° C.   The Weflo Figure 6001 and Figure 6001T offer the ultimate in reliable performance.
