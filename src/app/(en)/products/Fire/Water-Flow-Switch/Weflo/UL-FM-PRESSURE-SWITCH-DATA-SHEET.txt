UL / FM Waterflow Detectors For Systems That Use CPVC Fittings
The Model F6001U Waterflow Detector is used for wet sprinkler systems in 1” (25mm), 1.25” (32mm, 1.5” (40mm) or 2” (50mm) pipe size. The F6001U with CPVC adapter fits CPVC glued tees in these sizes. The F6001U with bronze adapter fits brass and copper threaded tees, in addition to 1” and 1.5” CPVC tees. Clearly marked plastic paddles (vanes) are included to fit the different sizes and fitting types utilized.
