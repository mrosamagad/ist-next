Description
Fireguard Mobile CO2 gas fire extinguishers are highly effective against fires involving electrical equipment like computers, photocopiers and generators. Carbon dioxide trolley with special discharge hose and horn to use in high reach hazardous area is also available.

Fireguard Mobile CO2 Extinguishers are suitable to use the areas like Flammable liquid storage, Food storage and processing plants, Airports, Telephone equipment rooms, Data processing centres, Engine rooms, Chemical plants and laboratories, Quench tanks, Petrochemical industries, Spray booths, etc.
